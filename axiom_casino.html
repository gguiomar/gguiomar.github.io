Axiom Casino

Jonathan Uliel Saldanha & Gonçalo Guiomar

Axiom Casino is an algorithmic fiction set in a world where chance is never neutral. Through shifting rooms governed by machine logic, it explores control, ritual, and collective behavior, turning the casino into a theatrical machine for thinking about power, belief, and the systems that influence our actions.

Theater Piece, Showing in Porto, 16–17 April 2026 — Portugal
