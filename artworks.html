  • Axiom Casino 2026

    Theater

    An algorithmic fiction set in a world where chance is never neutral. Through shifting rooms governed by machine logic, it explores control, ritual, and collective behavior, turning the casino into a theatrical machine for thinking about power, belief, and the systems that influence our actions. See the trailer here.

    Writing & direction: Jonathan Uliel Saldanha, Gonçalo Guiomar · Sound design: Jonathan Uliel Saldanha, Gonçalo Penas · Light design: Gonçalo Guiomar, Bárbara Paixão
