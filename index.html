Research

My research aims at uncovering the processes by which organisms learn to represent the world and the fundamental mathematical structures that support them. Trained as a physicist, I use a variety of tools and frameworks inherited from neuroscience, mathematics and machine learning to try to answer these questions whilst at the same time striving for biological realism and expositional simplicity. I've also had the chance to apply this knowledge in a series of art installations done in collaboration with various artists from around the world, in order to showcase how these intuitions might allow us to better understand ourselves.
